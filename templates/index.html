Speakers and Judges

We are proud to work with the best in the Blockchain industry.

  • Nadeem Mazen

    Nadeem is an award-winning serial entrepreneur and MIT graduate, with over a decade of experience in viral marketing and interactive software.

    Michelle Tsing

    Michelle is an attorney with expertise in SEC regulations. An entrepreneur herself, founding several technology companies, she now serves as advisor to startups with a special focus in Blockchain technology.

    Jamiel Sheikh

    Jamiel is CEO of Chainhaus, an advisory, software development, application studio and education company focused on blockchain, artificial intelligence and machine learning. Jamiel is an adjunct professor at Columbia Business School, NYU and CUNY teaching graduate-level blockchain, AI and data science subjects.

  • Babak Kia

    Babak is a talented engineer, crypto expert and investor. Aside from teaching Blockchain and software engineering at BU, he spends his time mentoring and advising startups in the Boston ecosystem.

    Jesse Li

    With a background in venture capital and blockchain, Jesse has been the strategic advisor to multiple crypto exchanges and funds. He is deeply involved in the early investment of many well-known blockchain projects.

    Chris Ba

    Co-founder of Ferryman Capital and UCLA graduate, Chris specializes in blockchain investment and listing. Deeply involved in the Asian crypto market, he has advised and aligned industrial resources for many first-tier blockchain startups.

  • Yazan Barghuthi

    Yazan is the Founder and CEO of the Jibrel Network - a startup focused on placing traditional financial assets on the Ethereum blockchain. Prior to founding Jibrel, Yazan worked in financial services across top tier management consultancies including Oliver Wyman and Deloitte, specializing in wealth and asset management. Yazan holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Chemical Engineering from the University College London.

    Navroop Sahdev

    Navroop Sahdev is a pioneering economist and futurist in emerging tech. She leads the Economics Strategy practice at The Fifty Five Foundry, building decentralized industry platforms that leverage state-of-the-art research in Economic Science. She is a fellow at MIT Connection Science and a 2017 United Nations Youth Delegate.
